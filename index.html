About

Hi, I'm Jade — a digital creative.

I'm a digital designer who believes great design should be beautiful and accessible — because experiences worth crafting are experiences worth sharing with everyone. (Big fan of the curb-cut mentality, and if you aren't familiar I'm always willing to talk about it! Please ask :) )

A true Renaissance woman, I don't clock out of creativity after work. I spin my own yarn on a drop spindle, crochet (which I've been doing for more than 20 years), and knit (sort of — my hands haven't figured this one out yet). Outside, I'm nurturing an herb garden and a soft spot for native wildflowers.

When I'm not designing, I'm competing. I'm a passionate West Coast Swing dancer who travels to hit the dance floor and compete! I'm equally at home on a hiking trail, around a table full of good food, or deep in conversation with people who light me up.

I share my life with a majestic Samoyed and three cats who tolerate my crafting projects with varying degrees of enthusiasm.
